# Spec: Practice Principle — Advice in Public

> Filled in during the second pass over backfilled specs. Status `draft`.

## Intent

State that architecture advice at Organization happens in the open: ADRs
over private memos, public PR reviews over private feedback, shared
forums over 1-1 escalations. Conversation is a learning multiplier;
private advice serves one team, public advice serves the organisation.

## Audience

- **Engineers and tech leads** considering whether to share advice.
- **Architecture group** modelling the public-first behaviour.
- **New joiners** orienting to Organization's communication norms.

## Success criteria

- [ ] Reader knows the default is **public**, not private.
- [ ] Reader can identify when private advice is acceptable.
- [ ] Reader sees the connection to [[decisions-stay-with-teams]] and
      [[architecture-advisory-forum]].

## Non-goals

- Banning private conversations.
- Mandating attendance at every forum.

## Open questions

- How to encourage public sharing without lengthening cycle times.

## Decision log

- **2026-05-20** — Made **public the default**, private the
  exception. Considered situational; rejected because situational
  norms drift toward private.

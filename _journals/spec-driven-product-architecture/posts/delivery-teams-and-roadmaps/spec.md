# Spec: Delivery, teams, and roadmaps

## Intent

Explain how Spec-Driven Product Architecture connects the domain model to delivery execution, team ownership, objectives, releases, targets, and roadmap overlays.

## Audience

Engineering leaders, product operations, product architects, delivery leads, and AI agents modeling product execution.

## Success criteria

- [ ] Reader sees delivery as part of product architecture, not a separate project-management layer.
- [ ] Reader understands how teams own product bricks and coordinate around capabilities.
- [ ] Reader can explain why roadmap and objectives should reference customers, KPIs, teams, capabilities, and bricks.
- [ ] Reader can follow the ride-sharing running example into ownership, release planning, and evidence.
- [ ] Reader understands generated documentation as a review surface for the operating model.

## Non-goals

- Prescribing one delivery methodology.
- Detailed release-planning template.
- Team topology tutorial.

# Spec: From customer value to architecture

## Intent

Show how Spec-Driven Product Architecture starts from customer value and translates it into architecture-relevant structure without losing the customer's language.

## Audience

Product architects, product managers, engineering leaders, and AI agents drafting customer and strategy models.

## Success criteria

- [ ] Reader understands why customer groups and JTBD come before product bricks.
- [ ] Reader can explain how KPI pyramids and strategy horizons make customer value operational.
- [ ] Reader can see how customer-facing structure creates constraints for delivery and architecture.
- [ ] Reader can follow the ride-sharing running example from customer value toward architecture.
- [ ] Reader avoids treating discovery and evaluation as in-product task steps.

## Non-goals

- Full customer modeling tutorial.
- Domain-specific customer research.
- Detailed KPI target calibration.

# Spec: Modeling diverse domains

## Intent

Explain why Spec-Driven Product Architecture uses many example domains, and how diversity helps distinguish reusable modeling structure from domain-specific product reality.

## Audience

Product architects, AI-agent operators, and repository maintainers who want examples to improve future domain modeling.

## Success criteria

- [ ] Reader understands why diverse domains are part of the approach, not demo filler.
- [ ] Reader can distinguish reusable model invariants from domain-specific content.
- [ ] Reader sees how examples help AI agents infer schema, naming, depth, and quality bar.
- [ ] Reader understands the risk of forcing every domain into the same product story.
- [ ] Reader can jump from representative generated domain pages to the matching source specification folders.

## Non-goals

- Deep comparison of all existing domains.
- Ranking domains by maturity.
- External market analysis for specific domains.

## Open questions

- Whether to name more domains or select three case-study domains for a later expansion.

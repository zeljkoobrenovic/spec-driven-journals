# Spec: What is spec-driven product architecture?

## Intent

Introduce the series and define the core thesis: spec-driven product architecture uses structured domain models as the durable interface between product strategy, implementation architecture, and AI-mediated authoring.

## Audience

Product architects, engineering leaders, product leaders, and AI-agent users who understand spec-driven development but need a broader product-domain frame.

## Success criteria

- [ ] Reader can distinguish this idea from ordinary spec-driven development.
- [ ] Reader can explain why product vision alone is not enough for AI agents.
- [ ] Reader can name the main specification layers: customers, strategy, delivery, capabilities, product bricks, teams, roadmap, and evidence.
- [ ] Reader can explain dreaming, exploring, and grounding as three modes of pragmatic product architecture.
- [ ] Reader understands how grounding connects the product-domain model to actual data signals and evidence.
- [ ] Reader can navigate to the rest of the series.

## Non-goals

- Detailed schema documentation.
- A tutorial for creating a full product domain.
- A decision record about whether to adopt the approach.

## Open questions

- Whether to add diagrams or generated images in a later pass.

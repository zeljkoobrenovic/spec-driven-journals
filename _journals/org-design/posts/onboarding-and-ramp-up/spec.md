# Spec: Onboarding and Ramp-Up

> Filled in during the second pass over backfilled specs. Status `draft`.

## Intent

State that Organization treats onboarding as a **structured ramp-up
system**, not a first-day orientation event. Every new hire receives
role context, team context, technical setup, a buddy, manager
check-ins, learning path, and 30-60-90 day outcomes. Owned by the
hiring manager and team, supported by people operations and
communities of practice.

## Audience

- **Hiring managers** owning new-hire onboarding.
- **Buddies** supporting ramp-up.
- **New hires** orienting to Organization.
- **People operations** maintaining the shared scaffolding.

## Success criteria

- [ ] Reader knows onboarding is a **system**, not an event.
- [ ] Reader can name the components (context, setup, buddy,
      check-ins, learning path, 30-60-90 outcomes).
- [ ] Reader knows the hiring manager owns the outcome.

## Non-goals

- Defining specific onboarding checklists.
- Replacing functional training.

## Open questions

- How to measure ramp-up effectiveness.

## Decision log

- **2026-05-20** — Made the **hiring manager** the owner.
  Considered shared ownership with HR; rejected because shared
  ownership produces no ownership.

# Spec: Staff-Plus Technical Leadership

> Filled in during the second pass over backfilled specs. Status `draft`.

## Intent

State that Organization defines staff-plus engineering as a **real
technical leadership path**. Staff, principal, and equivalent
engineers create impact through cross-team technical direction,
architecture advice, mentoring, standards, complex problem solving,
and capability building. They **influence** decisions without
becoming hidden managers or approval gates.

## Audience

- **Engineers** considering the staff-plus path.
- **Senior leadership** sponsoring staff-plus engineers.
- **Engineering managers** working with staff-plus engineers.
- **Hiring** calibrating staff-plus level.

## Success criteria

- [ ] Reader can name the **scope** of staff-plus impact (cross-team
      direction, advice, mentoring, standards, problem solving).
- [ ] Reader knows staff-plus is **not** hidden management.
- [ ] Reader knows staff-plus is **not** an approval gate.

## Non-goals

- Defining exact staff-plus level rubrics.
- Replacing management for people decisions.

## Open questions

- How to balance staff-plus influence with team ownership.

## Decision log

- **2026-05-20** — Made staff-plus **influence-based**. Considered
  giving staff-plus approval authority; rejected because that
  conflicts with team ownership.

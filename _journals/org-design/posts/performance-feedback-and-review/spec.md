# Spec: Performance Feedback and Review

> Filled in during the second pass over backfilled specs. Status `draft`.

## Intent

State that Organization uses **continuous performance feedback with
periodic calibrated reviews**. Managers set expectations, collect
evidence, give timely feedback, support growth, address gaps early.
Formal reviews summarise a body of evidence — they must **not** be
the first time a person hears important feedback.

## Audience

- **Engineering managers** running the cycle.
- **Engineers** receiving and giving feedback.
- **Skip-level managers** in calibration.
- **HR / people operations** running calibrations.

## Success criteria

- [ ] Reader knows formal reviews **summarise** a body of evidence,
      not surprise people.
- [ ] Reader can name the cycle components (expectations, evidence,
      feedback, growth, accountability).
- [ ] Reader knows continuous feedback is the foundation.

## Non-goals

- Defining the rating scale.
- Replacing compensation framework.

## Open questions

- How to handle calibration fairness across teams.

## Decision log

- **2026-05-20** — Chose **continuous + calibrated** over annual
  surprise. Considered annual-only; rejected because surprise
  reviews destroy trust.
- **2026-05-20** — Required **evidence-based** reviews. Considered
  vibes-based; rejected because vibes-based reviews encode bias.

# Spec: Dual Career Paths and Transparent Progression

> Filled in during the second pass over backfilled specs. Status `draft`.

## Intent

State that Organization maintains **transparent dual career paths** for
individual contributors and managers with equal dignity. Progression
based on demonstrated scope, impact, judgement, collaboration, and
capability growth — not on becoming a manager by default. Staff-plus
technical leadership is a real path with expectations and
accountability.

## Audience

- **Engineers** evaluating progression options.
- **Engineering managers** sponsoring growth.
- **HR / people operations** running the career framework.
- **Hiring managers** matching levels across paths.

## Success criteria

- [ ] Reader knows IC and management paths have **equal dignity**.
- [ ] Reader can name the progression criteria (scope, impact,
      judgement, collaboration, capability).
- [ ] Reader knows management is **not the default** promotion path.

## Non-goals

- Defining specific level rubrics.
- Replacing the compensation framework.

## Open questions

- Concrete cross-walk between IC and management levels.

## Decision log

- **2026-05-20** — Chose **dual paths with equal dignity**.
  Considered manager-track as the primary promotion path; rejected
  because that pushes strong engineers into management against fit.

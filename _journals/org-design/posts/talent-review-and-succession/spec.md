# Spec: Talent Review and Succession

> Filled in during the second pass over backfilled specs. Status `draft`.

## Intent

State that Organization uses **lightweight talent review and succession
planning** for critical roles, capabilities, and leadership paths.
The process identifies continuity risks, growth opportunities,
capability gaps, and potential successors. **Transparent enough to be
fair, evidence-based enough to avoid politics, developmental rather
than a secret ranking exercise.**

## Audience

- **Senior leadership** running talent reviews.
- **Engineering managers** participating in reviews.
- **Engineers** affected by succession decisions.
- **HR / people operations** facilitating.

## Success criteria

- [ ] Reader knows talent review is **lightweight**, not annual
      bureaucracy.
- [ ] Reader knows it is **developmental**, not secret ranking.
- [ ] Reader can name what it identifies (continuity risks,
      opportunities, gaps, successors).

## Non-goals

- Creating a "high-potential" hidden tier.
- Replacing performance reviews.

## Open questions

- How much transparency is enough to be fair.

## Decision log

- **2026-05-20** — Made talent review **developmental**, not
  rank-based. Considered stack-ranking; rejected because rank-based
  systems destroy collaboration.

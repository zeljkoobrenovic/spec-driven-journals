# Spec: Product and Engineering Partnership Around Outcomes

> Filled in during the second pass over backfilled specs. Status `draft`.

## Intent

State that Organization organises product and engineering around **shared
outcomes**, not a demand-supply handoff. Product, design, engineering,
quality, and data work together in durable teams that **discover and
deliver solutions**. Leaders provide strategy, constraints, and
priorities; teams are accountable for solving the right problems.

## Audience

- **Product managers** working with engineering teams.
- **Engineering managers** partnering with PM.
- **Designers, QA, data** as full team members.
- **Senior leadership** providing strategy and constraints.

## Success criteria

- [ ] Reader knows product and engineering share **outcome
      accountability**, not a demand-supply handoff.
- [ ] Reader can name the disciplines on a durable team.
- [ ] Reader knows leaders provide **strategy, constraints,
      priorities** — not solutions.

## Non-goals

- Eliminating product or engineering roles.
- Defining specific PM frameworks.

## Open questions

- How to scope leadership input without dictating solutions.

## Decision log

- **2026-05-20** — Chose **shared accountability for outcomes**.
  Considered demand-supply; rejected because handoff models
  hollow out team ownership.

# Spec: Structured Hiring and Selection

> Filled in during the second pass over backfilled specs. Status `draft`.

## Intent

State that Organization uses **structured, role-related hiring** for
engineering roles. Every hiring process starts from a clear role need
and competency map, uses planned interviews with consistent criteria,
records evidence against a rubric, and makes hiring decisions through
**calibrated review**. Hiring is a product and organisation design
decision, not only a recruiting transaction.

## Audience

- **Hiring managers** running selection.
- **Interviewers** trained in structured interviewing.
- **Recruiters** managing the process.
- **HR / people operations** calibrating decisions.

## Success criteria

- [ ] Reader knows hiring is **structured and role-related**, not
      conversational.
- [ ] Reader can name the components (brief, competency map,
      interview plan, trained interviewers, rubric, calibrated
      decision).
- [ ] Reader sees hiring as **org design**, not just recruiting.

## Non-goals

- Defining specific interview questions.
- Replacing recruiter sourcing strategies.

## Open questions

- Interviewer training cadence and certification.

## Decision log

- **2026-05-20** — Made hiring **structured**. Considered
  conversational interviewing; rejected because unstructured
  interviews encode bias and produce worse hires.

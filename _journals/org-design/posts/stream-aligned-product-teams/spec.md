# Spec: Stream-Aligned Product Teams as the Default Unit

> Filled in during the second pass over backfilled specs. Status `draft`.

## Intent

State that Organization uses **durable stream-aligned product teams** as
the default unit of software engineering. A team owns a coherent
stream of value from discovery through build, release, operation,
learning, retirement. Temporary project teams and specialist handoff
queues may exist for specific transitions — but they are **not the
default** shape.

## Audience

- **Engineering leadership** sizing the org.
- **Product managers** working with teams.
- **Engineers** orienting to team ownership.
- **HR / org design** structuring the org chart.

## Success criteria

- [ ] Reader knows stream-aligned teams are the **default**.
- [ ] Reader can name the lifecycle they own (discovery → build →
      release → operation → learning → retirement).
- [ ] Reader knows project groups and handoff queues are
      **exceptions**, not defaults.

## Non-goals

- Banning project teams outright.
- Defining specific team boundaries.

## Open questions

- How to handle work that genuinely doesn't fit a stream.

## Decision log

- **2026-05-20** — Made stream-aligned teams **the default**.
  Considered project-based as default; rejected because project
  teams produce undowned legacy.

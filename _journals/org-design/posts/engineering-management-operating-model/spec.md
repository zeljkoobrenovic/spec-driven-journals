# Spec: Engineering Management Operating Model

> Filled in during the second pass over backfilled specs. Status `draft`.

## Intent

State that engineering management at Organization is a **distinct
leadership discipline** accountable for people growth, team health,
hiring, performance, delivery systems, organisational clarity, and
sustainable execution. Engineering managers partner with product and
technical leaders; they are **not** project administrators or default
technical decision makers.

## Audience

- **Engineering managers** at all levels.
- **Engineers** working with managers.
- **Senior leadership** evaluating manager performance.
- **HR / people operations** supporting the discipline.

## Success criteria

- [ ] Reader can name the manager's accountabilities.
- [ ] Reader knows the manager is **not** the project admin or
      default tech-decider.
- [ ] Reader sees the partnership shape with product and technical
      leaders.

## Non-goals

- Defining specific management practices in detail.
- Replacing performance management or hiring processes.

## Open questions

- Concrete signals of a manager doing the job well vs poorly.

## Decision log

- **2026-05-20** — Treated management as **a discipline**, not
  admin. Considered framing it as senior IC; rejected because
  conflating the two underserves both.

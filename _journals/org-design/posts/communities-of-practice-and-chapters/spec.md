# Spec: Communities of Practice and Chapters for Shared Craft

> Filled in during the second pass over backfilled specs. Status `draft`.

## Intent

State that Organization uses **communities of practice and chapters** to
preserve shared craft across durable product teams. Communities
advise, teach, curate standards, and maintain reusable knowledge.
They **do not** replace product teams, create hidden reporting lines,
or become delivery queues.

## Audience

- **Engineers** participating in a community.
- **Community leads** running the cadence.
- **Engineering managers** sponsoring time for community work.
- **Architecture group** evaluating overlap with the AAF.

## Success criteria

- [ ] Reader knows communities **advise and teach**, not deliver.
- [ ] Reader can distinguish "community" from "team" or "reporting
      line".
- [ ] Reader sees connections to [[backend-guild]], [[frontend-guild]],
      [[mobile-guild]], [[genai-guild]].

## Non-goals

- Creating reporting lines through communities.
- Replacing product team accountability.

## Open questions

- How communities and chapters differ in practice.

## Decision log

- **2026-05-20** — Made communities **advisory only**. Considered
  giving them standards-setting authority; rejected because that
  recreates approval boards.

# Spec: Goal Setting and Planning Cadence

> Filled in during the second pass over backfilled specs. Status `draft`.

## Intent

State that Organization uses a **lightweight goal-setting cadence** that
connects strategy, product outcomes, engineering health, and team
work. Goals express **outcomes and constraints**, not task lists.
Teams use a small number of objectives, review progress regularly,
and **separate goal learning from individual performance scoring**.

## Audience

- **Engineering and product leadership** setting goals.
- **Team leads** translating goals into team work.
- **Engineers** working against the goals.
- **HR / people operations** keeping goals separate from performance.

## Success criteria

- [ ] Reader knows goals are **outcomes**, not task lists.
- [ ] Reader knows goals are **separate from individual performance
      scoring**.
- [ ] Reader can identify the cadence (company / domain / team
      levels).

## Non-goals

- Mandating an OKR framework specifically.
- Replacing existing strategic planning.

## Open questions

- Exact review cadence (quarterly, biannual?).

## Decision log

- **2026-05-20** — Separated **goals from performance scoring**.
  Considered tying goal attainment to performance; rejected because
  that incentivises sandbagged goals.

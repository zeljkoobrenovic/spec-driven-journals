> Filled in during the second pass over backfilled specs. Status `draft`.

after the second improvement pass. Verdict: ready for controlled
execution, not final enterprise standardization. Remaining gaps are
**execution evidence** (baselines, pilot artifacts, registry rows,
tested controls, policy sign-off, cost data) — not writing quality.

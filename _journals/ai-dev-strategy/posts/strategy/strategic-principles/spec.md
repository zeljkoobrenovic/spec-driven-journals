# Spec: Strategic Principles for AI in Software Modernization

> Filled in during the second pass over backfilled specs. Status `draft`.

## Intent

State **six principles** for using agentic coding as a modernization
accelerator: align AI with target architecture, move incrementally,
govern outputs, standardize before automation, choose tools
explicitly, manage agent capabilities as owned platform assets. The
mental shift: **the agent should follow the engineering system**, not
invent a new one.

## Audience

- **Engineers and tech leads** new to agentic coding.
- **AI Engineering Enablement** designing workflows.
- **Reviewers** evaluating AI proposals against the principles.

## Success criteria

- [ ] Reader can list the six principles.
- [ ] Reader knows the mental shift: agent follows the system.
- [ ] Reader can apply the principles to evaluate a proposal.

## Non-goals

- Defining specific tools (covered in [[tooling-decision]]).
- Replacing the broader [[ai-policy]].

## Open questions

- Whether six is the right count.

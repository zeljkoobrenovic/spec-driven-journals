# Spec: Introduction — AI Development Strategy

> Filled in during the second pass over backfilled specs. Status `draft`.

## Intent

Frame the AI development strategy as a **modernization strategy
first, tooling strategy second**. Make clear the goal is to make
modernization **repeatable, safe, and faster** — not to maximise AI
activity or generated code. Agentic coding matters because it can
turn legacy understanding, tests, standards, and governance into
repeatable engineering workflows.

## Audience

- **Engineering leadership** scanning the strategy.
- **New readers** orienting before deeper sections.
- **Reviewers** evaluating whether the strategy frame is right.

## Success criteria

- [ ] Reader knows this is a **modernization** strategy, not an AI
      strategy.
- [ ] Reader can state the goal (repeatable, safe, faster).
- [ ] Reader knows AI activity / generated code is **not** the metric.

## Non-goals

- Maximising AI adoption.
- Defining specific AI workflows (covered in later sections).

## Open questions

- Whether to add an explicit "what is not modernization" note.

## Decision log

- **2026-05-20** — Framed it as **modernization-first**. Considered
  AI-first; rejected because that incentivises activity over outcomes.

# Spec: What Are Spec-Driven Journals?

## Intent

Introduce the journal series and define the core idea: durable writing in Spec-Driven Journals is treated as structured source that can be specified, authored, reviewed, generated, linked, and maintained over time.

## Audience

Readers who want to understand Spec-Driven Journals before authoring in it: technical leaders, writers, AI-agent users, and maintainers.

## Success criteria

- [ ] Reader can explain the difference between a journal, a post, a spec, and generated documentation.
- [ ] Reader understands why Spec-Driven Journals is intentionally small and source-first.
- [ ] Reader sees the series as an article collection, not as a decision log.
- [ ] Reader knows where the rest of the series goes.
- [ ] Reader can follow the recommended order from philosophy to authoring mechanics to implementation.
- [ ] Reader can immediately find the official project repository and generated journal site.
- [ ] Article includes a named illustration placeholder showing the source-build-publication model.

## Non-goals

- Detailed implementation walkthrough.
- Full authoring checklist.
- External comparison with publishing platforms.

## Modalities

Which docs this spec drives beyond the main article (`index.md`).

- [x] `summary.md` — management summary
- [x] `dialog.md` — two-host dialog
- [x] `comics.md` — explainer comic

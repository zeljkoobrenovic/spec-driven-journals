What technology leadership looks like when AI does the heavy lifting — in eight panels.

![Comic panel: a robot instantly prints diagrams, memos, and roadmaps while the leader wonders what her job is now.](assets/images/prepare-for-ai-future/comic-01-instant-artifacts.jpeg)
**Panel 1:** *The artifacts that once took leaders hours now take seconds — which forces the real question: where does leadership value live?*

![Comic panel: a display case with a compass, scale, and megaphone labeled agency, judgment, and persuasion.](assets/images/prepare-for-ai-future/comic-02-three-skills.jpeg)
**Panel 2:** *Agency, judgment, persuasion: the three skills that define leadership impact when routine work is automated.*

![Comic panel: a desk divided between the robot's drafts and the leader's accountability stamp.](assets/images/prepare-for-ai-future/comic-03-accountability-line.jpeg)
**Panel 3:** *AI can suggest options, risks, and narratives. It cannot be responsible for consequences — accountability stays human.*

![Comic panel: workers wait on a 'waiting for mandate' bench while the leader builds a small pilot with the robot.](assets/images/prepare-for-ai-future/comic-04-agency.jpeg)
**Panel 4:** *Agency: the era of waiting is over. Leaders start bounded experiments and create the evidence for real decisions.*

![Comic panel: the robot fans out ten plausible plans while the leader checks one against hidden context questions.](assets/images/prepare-for-ai-future/comic-05-judgment.jpeg)
**Panel 5:** *Judgment: AI makes plausibility cheap. The leader's value is knowing which option survives this team, this risk, this history.*

![Comic panel: the leader replaces technical jargon on a flip chart with a business outcome and wins over executives.](assets/images/prepare-for-ai-future/comic-06-persuasion.jpeg)
**Panel 6:** *Persuasion: being right is not enough. Direction becomes a decision when stakeholders can evaluate it in their own terms.*

![Comic panel: a six-station weekly practice loop on a whiteboard, with one rejected option in a labeled bin.](assets/images/prepare-for-ai-future/comic-07-practice-loop.jpeg)
**Panel 7:** *The one-week loop: pick a real decision, demand three options, reject one in writing, stress-test risks, rewrite, get feedback.*

![Comic panel: the leader gathers colleagues and the robot around one shared plan, a framed mammoth picture on the wall.](assets/images/prepare-for-ai-future/comic-08-drawing-people-together.jpeg)
**Panel 8:** *The work changes, the job remains: connect technology with people, outcomes, and change — and make everyone smarter.*

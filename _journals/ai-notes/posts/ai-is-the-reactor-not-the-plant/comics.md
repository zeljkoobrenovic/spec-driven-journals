An eight-panel explainer comic for *AI Is the Reactor, Not the Plant*: generative AI is raw power like a reactor core, but almost all the value and engineering is the plant built around it to control, channel, and contain that power.

![Comic panel: a robot proudly holds a small glowing orange core labeled AI MODEL while an engineer stands unimpressed.](assets/images/ai-is-the-reactor-not-the-plant/comic-01-the-glowing-core.jpeg)
**Panel 1:** *Generative AI is raw power — a glowing core. Dazzling, and on its own, not yet useful.*

![Comic panel: an engineer taps a glowing core with a wrench next to a thermometer; the robot looks deflated.](assets/images/ai-is-the-reactor-not-the-plant/comic-02-just-a-boiler.jpeg)
**Panel 2:** *A reactor core is, plainly, 'the heat source for the power plant, just like the boiler is for a coal plant' (U.S. NRC).*

![Comic panel: a bare glowing core radiates wild heat lines while a nearby lightbulb stays dark.](assets/images/ai-is-the-reactor-not-the-plant/comic-03-uncontrolled-is-useless.jpeg)
**Panel 3:** *Uncontrolled, the core is both useless and a hazard. Control is what makes a reactor a reactor.*

![Comic panel: a cutaway plant where a tiny glowing core sits dwarfed by cooling, turbine, and containment machinery.](assets/images/ai-is-the-reactor-not-the-plant/comic-04-mostly-not-the-core.jpeg)
**Panel 4:** *Most of a plant — cost, components, engineering — is NOT the reactor. The marvel in the middle is a minority line item.*

![Comic panel: three vignettes around a core labeled control, channel, and contain.](assets/images/ai-is-the-reactor-not-the-plant/comic-05-control-channel-contain.jpeg)
**Panel 5:** *The plant does three jobs: control the rate, channel raw output into useful work, contain the damage when it fails.*

![Comic panel: a core wrapped in several concentric colored shields; the robot pokes the outer one while an inner holds.](assets/images/ai-is-the-reactor-not-the-plant/comic-06-defense-in-depth.jpeg)
**Panel 6:** *Defense in depth: no single guardrail. Assume each safeguard fails, and design the next one to still hold.*

![Comic panel: a robot in a hazmat suit aims a fire extinguisher at a laptop while an engineer holds a sign reading NOT a bomb.](assets/images/ai-is-the-reactor-not-the-plant/comic-07-where-it-breaks.jpeg)
**Panel 7:** *Mind the limits: a model can't melt down or explode. The analogy is about structure, not physical danger.*

![Comic panel: an engineer and a calm robot stand before a complete humming plant with a lit lightbulb, giving a thumbs-up.](assets/images/ai-is-the-reactor-not-the-plant/comic-08-the-plant-is-the-work.jpeg)
**Panel 8:** *AI handed us a reactor core. The work ahead is the plant — and that is the revolution.*
